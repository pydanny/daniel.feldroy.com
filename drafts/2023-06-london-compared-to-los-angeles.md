## Weather

In Los Angeles I spent most of my time in short trousers, often in sandals for days on end. Here it is June and I'm still in long trousers and a jacket.

Outside of stormy LA winters, London has a lot more rain. The cloud cover means that the sun is rarely seen. As we're higher up in latitude that means the days are a bit longer, which I enjoy.

## Smoking

Smoking is much more common in London. It's like stepping back decades in time. Vaping is really common, but burning tobacco is frequent too. A lot of parents smoke around children.

## Playgrounds

London wins out with playgrounds. There are a lot more of them, and they are much more interesting. They are also much more crowded. I've never seen a playground in London that wasn't full of children.
