We're going to be here for a while, so I thought I'd write a bit about why we moved, and what we're doing here.

# Why London?

There's so many reasons why we moved to London, but here's a few:

First off is the accessibility to the rest of England, Europe, and the world. We're a 2-to-3 hour train ride from Paris, and a 2 hour flight from most of Europe. We're also a 6 hour flight from the East Coast of the US, and a 10 hour flight from the West Coast. This makes it easy to travel to see family and friends, or for them to see us. 

Second is the employer. I'm working for [Kraken Tech :squid:](https://kraken.tech), part of [Octopus Energy Group :octopus:](https://octopusenergy.group). The home office is in London, and most of the people I interact with are in the GMT timezone. Moving to London has done wonders for my sleep schedule.

Third is the walking culture. We're able to walk to the grocery store, the park, and the train station. All this walking has been a boon to our fitness and soundness of mind. 

Fourth is the public transit of London/England/Europe, which is fast, dependable, and clean compared to where we are from. In London everything feels just 30 minutes away from everything else. Paris is less than three hours away. Getting out of any city is a 45 train ride. In other words, we're not going to miss having a car for a while.

Fifth are the friends I have in London and Europe. It's nice to see them outside of calls and conferences, to get to spend tim to with them in person. The compactness of the city compared to Los Angeles makes this a lot easier.

# What about the USA?
