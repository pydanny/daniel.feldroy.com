Better late than never! Here's my annual recap!

## Recap of 2025

- We moved from London, UK to Manila, Philippines
- Spent another year with my soul mate, Audrey. This year we spent much of it in one of her homelands, the Philippines
- Uma spent her 6th year on Planet Earth. She's really growing, spent the year focusing on school, goofy kid play, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, swimming, engineering, and science. Working on science kits and activities with her is one of my greatest joys. I also became a coach at her BJJ school; my blue belt helps validate my role there. She's quite the character and we adore her
- Suffered a serious back injury (herniated disc) from some bad lifts that had me bedridden for the first two months of the year. While I'm recovered from the injury I'm still struggling at times with sciatica. What's been effective for my recovery has been acupuncture, anti-inflammatories, stretching, mobility exercises, and living in a warm country. I still do BJJ, but am careful about partners and restrict my sparring to just 2-3 rolls per session
- Started doing a lot more gi BJJ
- Spent much of the year in the Philippines. I appreciate the people - individuals in general are friendly, hard-working, pragmatic, and trustworthy. The nation has come a long way economically in the 14 years I've been visiting
- Left an AI lab and have been focusing on startups and FOSS
- Started the [Air web framework](https://airwebframework.org/) with [Audrey](https://audrey.feldroy.com/)
- [Rewrote this blog in Air](https://github.com/pydanny/daniel.feldroy.com)
- [Spoke at PyCon Davao 2025](https://pycon-davao.durianpy.org/) and various meetups
- Snorkeled for the first time in nearly 20 years. I did it in Mexico back in 2006, and this time in the Philippines in Batangas. It was amazing to see the coral and fish life up close again
